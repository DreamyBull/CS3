    More About Us:

  • We are an International Marble Company originating from Philippines, General Santos City. We started as a local company owned by Ramesh Z. Robles for 15 years. It really started getting off when the inheritor of the company CEO Ralph Ramrick N. Robles started selling marble out of the city causing the marble company to go national. After 4 years Robles Rubble(Company Name) started exporting marble from the Philippines to China, South Korea, Malaysia, Indonesia, and Japan, causing it to become an internationally eventually reaching Europe And South America.

      • What do we sell?

  • We sell all sorts of marble starting from the most common type such as Carrara Marble to the highest quality marble such as Calacatta Marble and even Lustrous white Makrana marble (famously used in the Taj Mahal). To learn more about the marble we sell go to our Products page.
