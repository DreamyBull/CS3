Creepy Nuts

    Creepy Nuts is a Japanese J-Pop unit composed of two members R-Shitei, and DJ Matsunaga.

    I found out about Creepy Nuts while watching an anime called Call of the night and I fell inlove with the opening song so I started listening to them.

    My two favorite songs from Creepy Nuts are:

  • Yofukashi no Uta
  • Datenshi
  • Daten
